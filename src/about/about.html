Gabby is a UX Researcher from New York City. She works on IBM's CIO Design team to create generative AI experiences that help global teams work better together.

She also works on several other initiatives at the intersection of human-computer interaction and generative AI. She leads the IBM Explainability group to build new UX research methods that enable designers to create the next generation of AI products that are safe, effective and transparent. Additionally, her recent collaboration with the IBM Research HCAI team established the first set of design guidelines for generative AI products, published at CHI 2024.

When not UX-ing or AI-ing (or sometimes both!), you can find Gabby teaching her bunny new tricks or perusing the shelves of a used bookstore.
